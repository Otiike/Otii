About

Goshen Blessing was founded on 2021 just a year after the Covid-19 pandemic. The main objective for the formation of this organization is to reach out to the less vulnerable people in our Kenya society and God willing extend a further hand to the whole of East African Community. Our focus is majorly about the African Child “Mtoto Mwafrika”. This is by ensuring their educational needs are catered for and their basic need like food and clothing which has quite not affordable to the African Child.

Achievements

We have managed to visit so far three childrens home. Our first Childrens home to visit was the Lords servant Childrens Home located at Liyavo village, Kitale on August 2021.Our next visit was a childrens home in Kakamega and our third visit was a childrens home at Checkpoint, Webuye. We distributed to them school items to ensure easy learning for them. Food and clothing. We also engage with them to hear and assist with some personal issues they are going through as well as check on their mental wellbeing. We also engage with them in some outdoor activities like playing games among others.

Apart from extending our hand to African children, we also visited the less fortunate families in Siaya and we shared with them food stuff and clothing. We were so happy to get their blessings.

We are looking forward to reaching out to as many children as we can and helping them with their physical challenges and needs and also their mental issues.

Funding Model

Our main source of funds comes from each of us members of the organization where everyone is asked to contribute any monetary amount they can get. We also accept contribution in terms of food items and even clothing both new and used. We reach out to our friends for their support and invite them to join us too during the visits. We also get support from well-wishers who appreciate our efforts.

As a transparent organization, we account for every coin used via the treasurer.
